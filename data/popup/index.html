Remote YouTube

How to ?

To connect to Remote YouTube:

  1. Click onto the logo
  2. Open youtube app on your phone
  3. Select cast button (top right, next to notification icon)
  4. Choose "Yotube on TV" (If you are on the same wifi with the pc) or "Link with TV code"
  5. For more detail, see YouTube TV Help

Notice: For most of the time, you only need to pair your phone once.
